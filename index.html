Why Choose Casa Mezca

  • Close to shopping, dining, and medical services

Experience Paradise

Mezcales is a charming village nestled in the Riviera Nayarit, offering a mix of local authenticity, tropical landscapes, and proximity to beautiful beaches.

Mezcales stands as a vibrant residential community that blends traditional Mexican spirit with modern comforts and is just a 15-minute drive from Puerto Vallarta's international airport, making it highly accessible for visitors and residents alike.
